How does Yoja work?

Yoja monitors your GDrive and creates a private and confidential index of the contents. This index enables AI Search and AI Chat of your GDrive contents

AI Search enables you to search your GDrive contents with a natural language interface, for example, 'list all the powerpoint files that include the 2024 second quarter sales figures'

AI Chat enables generative AI capabilities - you can say things such as 'Generate content for the board meeting highlighting 2024 sales figures'

No need to upload specific files before chatting. No reason to traverse the directory hierarchy in your treasured private files. You don’t have to remember to update Yoja when you add, modify, or delete files from your google drive or dropbox.

Rest assured, the data will remain secure, will not be shared with others for training or other purposes, and will be removed if for whatever reason you choose to unsubscribe from Yoja.

Upon initial signup, Yoja begins the process of reading your files and generating mathematical representations called embeddings. Later, these embeddings are used to find the paragraphs that are most relevant to your chat query. This process is resource and time intensive and could take hours or even days. The left sidebar contains the chat history. If you hover over the chat that you want to delete, three dots appear in the right edge of the chat. When you move your mouse over the three dots, you will see a popup with the menu items 'Rename', 'Export' and 'Delete'. You can delete that single chat using the 'Delete' menu item.

In the left sidebar, at the bottom, the username is displayed along with the profile picture if one is publicly available on Google. If you mouse over this section, a menu with two items appears. The items are 'Settings' and 'Sign Out'. The settions menu that pops up when you press 'Settings' displays software versions and has functionality to connect to 'Dropbox' and also to delete all chats.
